What We Do

We take a human-centered approach to innovation that draws from our focus and passion for Africa to integrate the needs of the people, the possibilities of technology, and the requirements for business success.

Mobile Development

Custom apps for your business or startup to drive customer engagement, automate processes, or simply bring your idea into reality. We build for iOS, Android and Windows Phone.

Web Development

We specialize in interactive web experiences with a strong focus on design. We use Node.js, MongoDB and responsive layouts. See our service 54sites for expert web design.

Product Design

Technology is defined by the end result of how it interacts with people. We can help make that interaction beautiful, professional and unique. Stand out from the competition with us.

Bluetooth Sensors

Low cost solutions to problems that require temperature, pressure, Ph, temperature or motion sensors.

Mobile Strategy

We can help you find the right plan on how to use mobile for growing your business. Marketing, platforms and features.

Training and Education

We provide expert training in iOS, Android and Node.js development. We also foster code school programs for young people.

About Us

We are a small group of African engineers and designers who are passionate about fostering entrepreneurship with great software.

Our Mission

To build apps and connected devices that push African entrepreneurs to new heights.

We strive to embody world-class quality and design - made in Africa. Our focus is singly on African entrepreneurs and small businesses - not by copying Silicon Valley, but rather by innovating products rooted in the cultural, linguistic, and economic realities that beautifully define Africa from the rest of the world. We are here to build for impact over all else.

Our Culture


Do More with Less

Always deliver more than expected. If the customer receives exactly what he or she expected, we have failed. We want to delight and impress you at every step.

Foster Education

Pursue growth and learning. By being mentors and educators to young people in our comminities, we underscore the process of making coding fun, human and imapactful to all.

Give Back

Support your community. We are only as strong as our communities and how we represent that is a reflection on us. This is why we strongly support and contribute open source software.

Think Bigger

Life is short, do stuff that matters. Our aim is always to look at the bigger picture and ship products that can scale to help the most people possible.

Create Fun

Love what you do. The only way to maintain the energy, drive and focus to change the world, is to turn your passion your work. We make fun all day!

User Experience is King

Less about the code, more about the people. It's all about the impact the software has on people that makes software special, not in the details of implementation.
